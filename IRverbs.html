IR verbs

Conjugating "-IR" in the present tense

"-IR" verbs

IR verbs are the second most common verb type in French.

When conjugating IR verbs, first cut off IR part and then add the ending.
These are the endings below.

Pronoun Ending
je is
tu is
il/ elle/ on it
nous issons
vous issez
ils/ elles issent

So, punir would be conjugated like this...

First take off the "ir" from punir

So, it becomes pun

Next, add the appropriate endings.

Pronoun Ending
je punis
tu punis
il/ elle/ on punit
nous punissons
vous punissez
ils/ elles punissent
